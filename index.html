What to expect

At Daystar Church, we want you to have a powerful and memborable Easter experience. We have multiple service times and locations to give you the best opportunity to join us. When you arrive at any of our campuses, you can expect to be greeted with a warm welcome, enjoy a complimentary cup of coffee and help from our team to find a seat and enjoy the service. During the service you will experience, engaging worship music, hear a story of real life change through vide and receive an encouraging message from Pastor Allen.

Make sure you plan to give yourself time to grab some coffee and find your seat before service begins. If you are a family joining us for the first time, plan to arrive a little early, so that we can help you get your kid(s) checked-in to Daystar Kids and give you time to meet the leaders and look around.

First Time Guests

If this is your first time to Daystar Church, we are so glad to have you! We hope you will feel welcome and comfortable at Daystar. At all of our campuses you can expect to find a casual and friendly environment, where our team is there to help you with everything from finding a parking spot to getting a great seat in the auditorium! When you arrive at any of our campuses, be sure to stop by the Welcome Center, so that we can give you a free gift.

Families

We want Easter to be a special event for the whole family. We want your child(ren) to experience Easter too, so that is why we have created high-energy Daystar Kids services where your kids will hear the Easter story specifically tailored to them. We will provide a clean and safe environment, and loving and fun-filled experience for your kid(s).
